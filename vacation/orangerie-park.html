Parc de l’Orangerie

Parc de l’Orangerie is Strasbourg’s classic city park: wide paths, a lake, lawns for picnics, and storks nesting in the trees.

How We Use It in Itineraries

  • Slow morning walks or jogs for travelers who like green spaces.
  • Family-friendly time for kids to run and explore.
  • Pairing with a visit to the nearby European Quarter for a half-day outside the old town.

If you’d like time built in for relaxing in parks, just mention it in the trip request form and we’ll adjust your schedule.
