Strasbourg Christmas Markets

Strasbourg is often called the “Capital of Christmas,” thanks to its historic markets, giant trees, and festive lights across the old town.

What to Expect

  • Dozens of wooden chalets selling ornaments, gifts, and local crafts.
  • Plenty of food stalls with gingerbread, pretzels, and hot mulled wine.
  • Decorated streets, light displays, and a huge Christmas tree on Place Kléber.

Our Christmas Market Magic package is built around this experience, but any winter trip can be customized to include evening market time.
