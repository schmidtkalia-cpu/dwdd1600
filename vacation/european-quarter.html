European Quarter

North-east of the old town, the European Quarter is home to institutions like the European Parliament and the Council of Europe. It feels completely different from the medieval center: glass, steel, and wide avenues.

Why Visit

  • See Strasbourg’s modern side and its role in European politics.
  • Walk or bike along the river Ill with views of contemporary architecture.
  • Combine with Parc de l’Orangerie for a half-day outside the old town.
