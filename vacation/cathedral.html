Strasbourg Cathedral

Strasbourg Cathedral dominates the skyline with its intricate Gothic façade and single spire. The surrounding square is the heart of the historic center and a perfect orientation point.

Why We Include It

  • Climb the tower for panoramic views over the city and the Rhine plain.
  • Step inside to see stained glass, vaulted ceilings, and the astronomical clock.
  • Use the Cathedral square as a hub to explore nearby streets and cafés.

Many of our packages include a half-day walking tour starting from the Cathedral and finishing in Petite France. Ask for a guided option if you prefer to explore with a local.
