About Your Planner

Hi! My name is Kalia! I am an avid traveler with a deep love for Strasbourg and the timeless charm of northeastern France. Over the years, I’ve wandered its quiet canals, climbed the cathedral tower at sunrise, lingered in cozy cafés, and explored the tucked-away corners that most people never stumble upon. Every visit has given me a new memory — a friendly conversation, a breathtaking view, or a peaceful moment along the river — and it’s those experiences that made me fall completely in love with the city. Strasbourg feels like home every time I return, and creating this site is my way of sharing that feeling with others. I hope it inspires you to explore its beauty, history, and warmth for yourself.
