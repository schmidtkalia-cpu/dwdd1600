Alsace Wine Route

South of Strasbourg, the Alsace Wine Route winds through villages like Obernai, Colmar, and Riquewihr. It’s a chain of vineyards, hills, and storybook streets.

How We Structure Wine Route Days

  • Base in Strasbourg or Colmar, then take day trips by car or tour.
  • Visit 2–3 villages per day rather than trying to see everything.
  • Include at least one tasting at a small, family-run winery.

Our Wine Route Retreat package is built for this region, but we can also add a single wine-route day to shorter city stays.
