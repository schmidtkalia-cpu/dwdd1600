Petite France

Petite France is Strasbourg’s most photogenic district: timber-framed houses leaning over quiet canals, flower boxes, and narrow cobblestone streets. It’s where most visitors fall in love with the city.

In our itineraries, we typically schedule time here around golden hour, when the light reflects off the water and the narrow streets are at their calmest. The atmosphere feels like stepping straight into a fairy tale.

What You’ll Do Here

  • Stroll along the canals and watch the river locks in action.
  • Stop at a winstub (traditional Alsatian restaurant) for tarte flambée.
  • Cross the covered bridges for panoramic views of the district.
  • Enjoy evening walks when the houses and bridges are beautifully lit.
