About Me

I’m Kalia Schmidt, a designer who enjoys turning ideas into visuals that feel thoughtful, organized, and a little bit playful. I’m especially interested in editorial design, branding, and illustration.

Design philosophy

My work usually starts with a simple question: What story are we trying to tell? From there, I use typography, color, and layout to make that story clear and engaging.

  • Clarity first. Readers should never be confused about what to look at.
  • Details matter. Alignment, spacing, and hierarchy make or break a design.
  • Emotion counts. Color and imagery set the tone before a single word is read.

Tools I use

Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop

InDesign
The heart of my editorial work: magazines, brochures, and multi-page layouts.
Procreate / Drawing tools
